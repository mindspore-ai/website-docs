Release Notes

MindSpore Elec 0.2.0-rc1 Release Notes

Major Feature and Improvements

  • [STABLE] AD_FDTD: Support end-to-end differentiable FDTD simulation.

  • [STABLE] Maxwell3D: Support 3D frequency electromagnetic equation simulation with PINNs method.

  • [STABLE] pinnFWI: Support wave equation simulation with PINNs method.

  • [STABLE] gprMax: Support data-driven GPR electromagnetic inversion.

  • [STABLE] metasurface: Support Metasurface Hologram design with Physics-assisted GAN Network.
